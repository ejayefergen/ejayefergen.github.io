About Me

Hi, I’m Emma.

I’m a Product Manager who found my way here through data. I like understanding how things work, spotting patterns, and turning complexity into products that feel simple and useful.

A lot of my recent work has lived at the intersection of data, AI analytics, and experimentation, and I’m happiest when I’m learning something new or building in spaces that are still taking shape.

I care deeply about working with thoughtful people, staying curious, and making things that genuinely help others do their work a little more easily.

Outside of work, I spend as much time as I can outdoors, cook for the people I love, read constantly, and have a soft spot for Tolkien, The Great British Bake Off, misbehaved dogs, and anything topped with melted cheese.
